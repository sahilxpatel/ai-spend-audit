# Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Product Positioning
**Tagline:** Stop overpaying for AI. Audit your team's stack in 60 seconds.
**Target Audience:** Bootstrapped/seed-stage startup founders and engineering managers who are feeling the squeeze of multiplying $20/mo subscriptions across their team.

## Distribution Channels

### 1. Product Hunt Launch (Primary)
- **Timing:** Tuesday or Wednesday at 12:01 AM PST.
- **Hook:** "AI tools are eating your runway. We built a free deterministic engine to find out exactly where you are overpaying."
- **Assets Needed:**
  - High-contrast, dynamic OG image.
  - Video walkthrough showing a 60-second audit.
  - "Maker's Comment" highlighting the transparency of our deterministic engine.

### 2. X (Twitter)
- **Format:** Thread breaking down how a hypothetical team of 5 wastes $3,000/year on overlapping Copilot, ChatGPT, and Claude subscriptions.
- **CTA:** Link to the free audit tool.

### 3. Engineering Communities (IndieHackers, HackerNews)
- **Format:** "Show HN: We built an engine to find redundant AI spend in startup teams."
- **Focus:** Technical discussion on our open pricing database and deterministic logic.

## Conversion Funnel
1. **Acquisition:** User sees the OG Image / Twitter post showing "$1,200/yr saved".
2. **Activation:** User completes the 60-second form on our landing page.
3. **Retention/Lead Gen:** User is shown extreme value (their exact overspend). If >$500/mo, they are prompted to book a consultation. Otherwise, they opt-in for "Optimization Alerts."
