# KPIs and Metrics Tracking

To evaluate the success of the AI Spend Audit MVP, we will track the following metrics:

## 1. Top of Funnel
- **Unique Visitors:** Monitored via Vercel Web Analytics.
- **Form Start Rate:** Percentage of visitors who click "Add Tool".

## 2. Core Conversion
- **Audit Completion Rate:** Percentage of users who finish the form and view their results page.
- **Average Computed Savings:** The mean dollar amount our engine identifies as wasteful spend.

## 3. Lead Generation (Bottom of Funnel)
- **Email Capture Rate:** Percentage of completed audits that result in an email submission.
- **Consultation Request Rate:** Percentage of high-savings users who explicitly request a consultation.

## 4. Viral Loop
- **Share Rate:** Tracked by UTM parameters on the `/audit/:id` public share links.
- **K-Factor:** How many new unique visitors does each shared audit generate?
